Install It Now

Copy this snippet and paste it anywhere on your website.

<!-- Tattoo Price Estimator Widget -->
<script src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/Yarmoluk/shop-estimator@latest/engine.js"
  data-config="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/Yarmoluk/shop-estimator@latest/configs/tattoo.json"
  data-shop="Your Shop Name"
  data-minimum="100"
  data-hourly="150"
  data-accent="#e94560"
  data-booking="https://your-booking-link.com"></script>
Attribute What It Does Default
data-shop Your shop name displayed in the widget header "Your Tattoo Shop"
data-minimum Your shop minimum price $80
data-hourly Your hourly rate for estimates $150
data-accent Brand color (hex code) for buttons and highlights #e94560
data-booking URL to your booking/consultation page Hidden if empty
